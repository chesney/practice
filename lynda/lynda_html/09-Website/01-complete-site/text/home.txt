<h2>Latest News</h2>
<h4>Simon Craven wins the Maui invitational.</h4>
<p>
Late July saw some of the best surfing the world has ever seen. The water temperature and currents brought
perfect conditions for the Maui Invitational and the field wasted no time in shredding last year's scores. When
the dust settled Simon Craven won his first ever Maui title. Fresh off his win in Australia, it looks like
Simon's a name we'll be hearing a lot of in the future!
</p>
<h4>Velosurty releases new board</h4>
<p>
Up and coming surf shop Velosurty released a their new long board last month. &ldquo;Keerang!&rdquo; echo's the
super-hero theme of their last long board, &ldquo;Bang-Pow!&rdquo; Artist Kelly Crandle supplies the look and
feel. This board guarantees a smooth ride and eye-catching looks. In stores next month. While you're at it,
check out our
<a href="gear/boards.htm">new boards</a>
as well!
</p>
